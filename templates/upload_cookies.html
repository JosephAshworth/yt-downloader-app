How to get your YouTube cookies:

  1. Install a browser extension: Get "Get cookies.txt" for Chrome or "Cookie Quick Manager" for Firefox
  2. Go to YouTube: Make sure you're signed in to your Google account
  3. Export cookies: Use the extension to export cookies for youtube.com
  4. Save as .txt: Save the file as "cookies.txt"
  5. Upload here: Select the file and click Upload
⚠️ Important: Keep your cookies secure. They give access to your YouTube account.
